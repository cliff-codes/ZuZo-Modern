The "Admin Trap" – The Price Cap Reality

The NDIS Price Guide caps your revenue per participant. If your admin costs rise, your margin vanishes. Most providers are stuck paying Australian award rates ($35+/hr) for data entry, rostering, and invoice reconciliation.

Average Plan Manager spends 12 hours/week on non-billable admin work

The Math That Doesn't Add Up

When you hire a local FTE for admin support:

  • Base salary: $65,000–$75,000/year
  • Superannuation (11%): $7,150–$8,250/year
  • Desk, software, benefits: $5,000–$8,000/year
  • Total Annual Cost: $77,150–$91,250

And even then, your staff works only 9-5. Invoices that arrive at 4:55 PM sit until tomorrow morning.

The Real Cost of Backlogs

Every delayed invoice is revenue delayed. In the NDIS space, cash flow is survival. Plan Managers who process faster build reputation. Those with backlogs lose participants to competitors.
