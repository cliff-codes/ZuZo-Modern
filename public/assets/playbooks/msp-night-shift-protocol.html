Why Your Engineers Are Leaving

You pay your Level 3 engineers $100,000+ annually to solve complex architectural problems, optimize cloud infrastructure, and mentor junior staff. But at 2 AM, they wake up to a PagerDuty alert. A printer spooler is stuck. A user forgot their password. A backup failed.

They groggily reset the printer. They unlock the account. They restart the backup. Then they can't fall back asleep.

The Cost of Alert Fatigue

This is called "alert fatigue." Your senior engineers experience it weekly. After 18 months, they're burned out. By month 24, they've already updated their LinkedIn profile and accepted an offer from a competitor.

Replacing a senior engineer costs 150% of their annual salary. A $100k engineer costs $150,000 to replace (recruitment, training, lost productivity).

The Secondary Problem: Customer Satisfaction

When your on-call engineer is exhausted, response times degrade. A 3 AM ticket waits 45 minutes instead of 15 minutes. Customers notice. Your NPS score drops. Churn accelerates.

Why Traditional "Night Shift" Doesn't Work

You might think: "Hire a Level 2 in India or Eastern Europe for night shift."

The problem? It's night there, too. You're asking humans to work against their circadian rhythms. They're slower, make more mistakes, and burn out faster than your US team.

You need someone working during their daytime.

The Three-Layer Triage Model

Layer 1: Triage & Noise Filtering
We receive the alert. Is it a false alarm? Is it a monitoring misconfiguration? We close it without waking your team. This alone eliminates 20–30% of your overnight noise.
Layer 2: Remediation (Level 1–2 Tasks)
We execute your documented SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures). Password resets. User account additions. Backup verifications. Network reboots. Printer troubleshooting. All handled using your credentials, your process, your client communication templates.
Layer 3: Smart Escalation
Only P1/Critical events wake your US team. "Server is down." "Database is corrupted." "Security incident detected." These are rare (5–10 per week). Your engineers handle the strategic work. Routine tickets? Handled overnight.

The Ticket Workflow

  1. Client creates ticket in your PSA (e.g., ConnectWise)
  2. Our Ghana team auto-receives it via API
  3. We resolve 70–80% autonomously
  4. We escalate 20–30% to your on-call engineer (if necessary)
  5. You wake up to a solved queue, not a crisis
